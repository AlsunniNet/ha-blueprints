# 🏛️ AlsunniNet | Lights and Fans Control

The ultimate "Universal Bathroom" blueprint. This automation manages lighting and climate dynamically based on your available sensors.

## 🚀 Key Logic
- **Motion Trigger:** Lights and fans turn on instantly. 
- **Lux Sensitivity:** Lights will stay off if the room is already bright enough from sunlight.
- **Climate Safety:** Fan kicks in automatically if temperature or humidity exceeds your thresholds, even without motion.
- **Smart Clear:** After leaving the room, lights turn off quickly, but the fan stays on until the air is clear or your custom timer expires.

## ⚙️ Requirements
- **Required:** Motion Sensor(s), Light(s).
- **Optional:** Fan(s), Lux Sensor, Humidity Sensor, Temp Sensor.
